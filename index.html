Antonio Loquercio, PostDoc in Robot Learning. Incoming Assistant Professor at University of Pennsylvania

My research is situated at the intersection of robotics, artificial intelligence, and computer vision. My work is focused on crafting innovative solutions that can handle real-world challenges, such as autonomous navigation in unstructured environments, manipulation, and human-robot interaction. My solutions mainly leverage large-scale learning and data.

My overarching goal is to empower robots with the capability to learn, understand, and interact with their surroundings in a way that is both reliable and adaptable, ultimately making them an integral part of our personal and professional lives. I am now working as a PostDoc at BAIR with Prof. Jitendra Malik.
I got my doctoral degree at ETH and University of Zurich with the Robotics and Perception Group working with Prof. Davide Scaramuzza.

I am recruiting highly motivated and talented individuals to join my new lab! Please follow the instructions on this page to apply. In your application, please make sure to mention my name under faculty you are interested in working with.
