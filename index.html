Antonio Loquercio, PhD Candidate in Machine Learning for Robotics

Hi! I am a PhD Candidate at ETH and University of Zurich. I work at the intersection of robotics, computer vision and neuroscience at the Robotics and Perception Group under the supervision of Prof. Davide Scaramuzza.

Education

MSc in Robotics, Systems and Control.

ETH Zurich, 2017. My studies were funded by the ETH Excellence Scholarship program. I won the ETH Medal for outstanding master thesis.

BSc in Engineering Sciences

University of Rome Tor Vergata, 2015. My studies were funded by a scholarship from the Collegio Universitario Lamaro Pozzani.
